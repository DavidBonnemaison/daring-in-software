Is reality better ?

Not really, but :

  • They got to learn how to use a new valuable tool
  • In the end, the feature got easier to change
  • It helped them recruit new people
Here already, we can see that being daring was not "the right" choice, because there was no rationally good or bad choice. It was a decision, that led to more opportunities.
That means that we have to dare to think by ourselves (here, choosing an emerging technology over the established one), but when there is no "right" choice, we also need to dare to decide.
